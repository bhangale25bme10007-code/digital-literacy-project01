Stay Safe Online Checklist:

1. Never share OTP or PIN with anyone
2. Always verify payment requests before accepting
3. Use only official banking apps
4. Avoid clicking on unknown links
5. Enable app lock and security features
6. Do not use public WiFi for transactions
7. Regularly update passwords
8. Report fraud immediately on cybercrime.gov.in
9. Call helpline number 1930 in case of fraud
